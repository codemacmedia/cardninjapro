Frequently Ask Questions

01. How do I create Card Ninja Pro Account ?

Anyone can easily create an account with us by clicking the Sign Up button located on the top right of the page.

02. Where can I Contact the developers?

Most pages have a form located on the bottom to submit questions, however you can contact us by clicking on the Contact us page in the navigation bar.

03. How do I Start Playing ?

You can start playing our current games by clicking on game on the navigation bar located on the top of each page.

04. How do I Move Up a Level ?

The current model of Card Ninja Pro does not have a level up system, But will be implemented in the near future.

05. How do I support the developers?

Anyone can donate to the team at the bottom of each page. We also accept Bitcoin :)

06. Does Card Ninja Pro have its own game currency?

Ninja bucks will be in the next update coming soon.

07. How many developers are working on CNP?

Card Ninja Pro was built and developed by a four man coding team called "The Four Horsemen".

08. Can I add My Local Bank Account ?

This feature is coming soon
